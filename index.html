Decent results from BAC from a credit point of view marked by continued build-up in capital (Basel III at 9.4%, up 20bps sequentially) and progress towards resolving the legacy mortgage issues. Focus is now turning to improving profitability with cost cutting playing a major role against a backdrop of anemic top line growth. The stock market had higher expectations on cost cutting and FICC top line revenues, and sent the stock 6% lower on the day.
